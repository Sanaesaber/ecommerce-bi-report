# E-Commerce Business Intelligence Report

End-to-end BI project: RFM segmentation, cohort retention analysis, and Power BI dashboard.

## Business Problem
How can an e-commerce platform identify revenue drivers,
customer segments, and retention gaps?

## Results
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Net Revenue Analyzed | 2,447,867 MAD |
| VIP + Loyal Revenue Share | 50% |
| Month-1 Retention | Drops from 100% to 22% |
| Peak Season | Q4 confirmed |

## Tech Stack
Python · Pandas · RFM Modeling · Cohort Analysis · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Power BI

## Key Insights
- Top 20% of customers generate 68% of total revenue
- Q4 drives 38% of annual sales
- At Risk segment represents recoverable revenue
- Month-1 retention is the most critical drop-off point
